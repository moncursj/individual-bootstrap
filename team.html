Team


Charles P. Moncure, Jr., Partner

Mr. Moncure co-founded Dominion Partners in 1996. He has been active in working with middle market companies on capital raising and mergers and acquisition advisory assignments throughout his career. Mr. Moncure began his career in 1984 with NCNB, a predecessor organization to Bank of America. Originally based in the investment banking group in Charlotte, Mr. Moncure moved to Dallas following Bank of America's acquisition of First Republic Bank and established an investment banking operation, which eventually employed more than 65 professionals covering the southwest and western United States. He departed Bank of America in 1993 as a Senior Vice President and Director in the Corporate Finance Group, to join what is now BB&T Capital Markets in Richmond. Mr. Moncure is a graduate of Randolph-Macon College and earned an M.B.A. from Indiana University. He is Past President of the Rotary Club of Innsbrook and served as its Foundation Chair. He is Vice Chair of the Yellow Jacket Club Advisory Board at Randolph-Macon College, where he also served as a trustee and chaired both the Board of Associates and Presidents Society.

Frederick T. Naschold, Partner

Mr. Naschold, also a co-founder of Dominion Partners, L.C., has approximately 30 years of investment banking experience focused principally on middle-market companies in the mid-Atlantic and Southeast. His activities have included mergers and acquisitions, leveraged buyouts, private debt, mezzanine, and equity financings, as well as public offerings of a variety of securities. Mr. Naschold worked with predecessor organizations of BB&T Capital Markets and Wells Fargo Securities for twenty years before co-founding Dominion. He holds a B.S. in Business Administration from the University of Richmond and a Master of Business Administration from Emory University. Mr. Naschold is a charter member and past president of the Rotary Club of Short Pump. He served on the Virginia Home for Boys and Girls Board of Associates for over a decade and was a founding director and member of the executive committee of the University of Richmond Alumni Association.

Michael F. Bertolino, Jr., Analyst

Mr. Bertolino joined Dominion Partners in May 2018. He earned a Bachelor of Arts in business with a concentration in finance from Randolph-Macon College.
